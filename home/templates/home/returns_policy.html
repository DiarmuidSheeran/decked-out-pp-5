Returns Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchases from our trading card game ecommerce site. If you need to return a product, please review our returns policy below:

Returns Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following criteria:

  • The item is in its original packaging.
  • The item is unused and in the same condition as when you received it.
  • The return request is submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.

Refunds

Once we receive your returned item and inspect it, we will notify you of the status of your refund. If your return is approved, a credit will be applied to your original method of payment within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our returns policy or the return process, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are here to assist you.
